TopLoggerStats

About this application

This app allows you to look at your climbing achievements that you've logged in TopLogger. I started bouldering in September 2020, and I've been in love with it ever since. TopLogger's slogan "Let's get even more addicted" is something I fully embrace. This app is made to fulfill your thirst for data and insights during your climbing journey. Enjoy!


Privacy:

Technically, while this application uses cookies, it only saves the gyms you've requested and your TopLogger user ID. Nothing from your visits is shared with other parties. I realize this is quite rare these days, but I want to ensure your privacy. The data we store includes your ascents and rating, which contain your TopLogger user ID.

For the nerds:

I developed this app in Flask, using ApexCharts for the visuals. The back-end optimization emphasizes speed, with heavy reliance on Cython for speed improvement. SQL is the main data storage for the gyms, routes, ascents and ratings. I designed the front end using plain HTML and CSS, with a touch of Vanilla JS and jQuery.
